How to play

The original app on Android playstore has been deleted by the publisher due to guilt over what the publisher considered to be its addictive nature and overuse. however a website called FlappyBird.IO allows all devices with a browser and an internet connect to play the original game

The rules are simple, but the controls may vary between platforms

  • PC
    • Mouse or Space Bar to Flap

  • Any touch-screen enabled device
    • Tap to Flap
